flushleft

 \begin{flushleft}
 Text on line 1 \\
 Text on line 2 \\
 ....
 ....
 \end{flushleft}
The flushleft environment allows you to create a paragraph consisting of lines that are flushed left to the left-hand margin. Each line must be terminated with a \\.
